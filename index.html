About Me

Ernesto Garcia was born and raised in the small agriculture-dominant California town of Los Banos. He graduated from Los Banos High School as valedictorian and attended UCLA to pursue a degree in neuroscience. While at UCLA, he served the community as a volunteer clinic assistant and laboratory assistant.

After graduating from UCLA with a biological background, Ernesto decided to pursue a career in the tech industry. His first step on this journey was to teach himself iOS programming.

Ernesto enjoyed the coding experience so much that he decided to become a full-stack web developer. He is currently enrolled in the UC Davis Coding Bootcamp to fulfill this goal.
